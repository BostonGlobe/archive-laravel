2003 MIMC AWARDS

Circle takes home best in show at 2003 MIMC Awards

By Boston.com, 10/22/2003

The Massachusetts Interactive Media Council (MIMC) held its eighth annual MIMC Awards last night, recognizing achievements in the development and implentation of interactive technologies and applications.

ADVERTISEMENT

The event, held this year at the Boston Copley Place Marriott, was focused on building business and technology. Award finalists competed in 27 award categories, honoring Web sites, titles, online advertising campaigns, and emerging technologies.

2003 MIMC Award Winners:

Best of awards:

Best of Show: pfgoldfish.com, by Circle for Pepperidge Farms Goldfish

Best Creative Concept: Jordan's Furniture RoomPlanner, by Hookumu, Inc. for Jordan’s Furniture

Best User Experience: MIT OpenCourseWare, by MIT OpenCourseWare, Sapient Corporation, Microsoft, Akamai, William and Flora Hewlett Foundation and The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation

Best Use of Technology: Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts Member Self-Service Portal, by Molecular for Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts
