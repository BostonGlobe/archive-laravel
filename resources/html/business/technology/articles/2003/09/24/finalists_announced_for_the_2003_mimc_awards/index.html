Finalists announced for the 2003 MIMC Awards

9/24/2003

The Massachusetts Interactive Media Council, Inc. (MIMC) has announced the finalists for its Eighth Annual MIMC Awards, which recognize achievements in the development and implementation of interactive technologies and applications.

ADVERTISEMENT

This year’s theme is focused on building business with technology. The 2003 MIMC Awards will take place on Tuesday, October 21, at the Boston Copley Marriott. For more information of the event, visit www.mimcawards.org.
