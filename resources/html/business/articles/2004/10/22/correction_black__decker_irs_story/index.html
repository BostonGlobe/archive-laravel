Correction: Black & Decker-IRS story

October 22, 2004

BALTIMORE -- In an Oct. 21 story about a federal court ruling in a tax case involving Black & Decker Corp., The Associated Press erroneously reported Eileen O'Connor's job title. She is an assistant attorney general for the tax division of the Justice Department, not the tax division of the IRS.
