Business events scheduled for the coming week

By The Associated Press
April 17, 2009
  • Email|
  • Print|
  • Single Page|
  • |
Text size – +

SATURDAY, April 18

PORT-OF-SPAIN, Trinidad -- President Barack Obama and other leaders of 34 democratic nations in Western Hemisphere discuss regional issues at Summit of the Americas. Through April 19.

MONDAY, April 20

NEW YORK -- The Conference Board releases leading indicators for March, 10 a.m.

LAS VEGAS -- National Association of Broadcasters show. FCC chief Julius Genachowski to speak. Through April 23.

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- Bank of America Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

NATICK, Mass. -- Boston Scientific Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

INDIANAPOLIS -- Eli Lilly & Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

HOUSTON -- Halliburton Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

PAWTUCKET, R.I. -- Hasbro Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

ARMONK, N.Y. -- International Business Machines Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

DALLAS -- Texas Instruments Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

BEIJING -- International Atomic Energy Agency conference on nuclear energy days after inspectors from U.N. body removed all seals and switched off surveillance cameras at North Korea's main nuclear site in Yongbyon.

TUESDAY, April 21

WASHINGTON -- Senate Health, Education, Labor and Pensions Committee hearing on green skill training for workers; Joint Economic Committee hearing on systemic threats of large financial institutions; Senate Finance Committee hearing on overhauling the health care system; House Oversight and Government Reform subcommittee hearing on the Treasury Department's plan for toxic assets; House Judiciary subcommittee hearing, "A New Age for Newspapers: Diversity of Voices, Competition and the Internet."

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Advanced Micro Devices Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

MIDDLETOWN, Ohio -- AK Steel Holding Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- Arbitron Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- Bank of New York Mellon Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

MCLEAN, Va. -- Capital One Financial Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

PEORIA, Ill. -- Caterpillar Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

ATLANTA -- Coca-Cola Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

ATLANTA -- Delta Air Lines Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

WILMINGTON, Del. -- DuPont Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

MILWAUKEE -- Johnson Controls Inc. reports second-quarter financial results.

BETHESDA, Md. -- Lockheed Martin Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

WHITEHOUSE STATION, N.J. -- Merck & Co. MRK reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- New York Times Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

NORFOLK, Va. -- Norfolk Southern Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

KENILWORTH, N.J. -- Schering-Plough Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

OMAHA, Neb. -- TD Ameritrade Holding Corp. reports second-quarter financial results.

MINNEAPOLIS -- U.S. Bancorp reports first-quarter financial results.

ARLINGTON HEIGHTS, Ill. -- UAL Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

HARTFORD, Conn. -- United Technologies Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

MINNETONKA, Minn. -- UnitedHealth Group Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

ENGLEWOOD, Colo. -- Western Union Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Yahoo Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

WEDNESDAY, April 22

WASHINGTON -- International Monetary Fund releases its world economic outlook; Senate Foreign Relations Committee hearing on climate change; Senate Commerce, Science and Transportation Committee hearing on overhauling the Federal Communications Commission; Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs subcommittee hearing on waste and fraud in Medicare and Medicaid; House Science and Technology Committee hearing on measuring greenhouse gas emissions; House Transportation and Infrastructure subcommittee hearing on helicopter medical services; House Ways and Means Committee hearing on changing the health insurance market.

ORLANDO, Fla. -- AirTran Holdings Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

RICHMOND, Va. -- Altria Group Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

CUPERTINO, Calif. -- Apple Inc. reports second-quarter financial results.

DALLAS -- AT&T Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

CHICAGO -- Boeing Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

HOUSTON -- Continental Airlines Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- eBay Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW ORLEANS -- Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

DALLAS -- Kimberly-Clark Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- McDonald's Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- Morgan Stanley reports first-quarter financial results.

LOS ANGELES -- Northrop Grumman Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

SAN DIEGO -- Qualcomm Inc. reports second-quarter financial results.

RESTON, Va. -- SLM Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

INDIANAPOLIS -- WellPoint Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

SAN FRANCISCO -- Wells Fargo & Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Yum! Brands Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

THURSDAY, April 23

WASHINGTON -- Labor Department releases weekly jobless claims, 8:30 a.m.; National Association of Realtors releases existing home sales for March, 10 a.m.; Freddie Mac, the mortgage finance company, releases weekly mortgage rates; Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee hearing on state and local oversight of stimulus funding; House Education and Labor subcommittee hearing on reducing the cost of health insurance.

SEATTLE -- Alaska Air Group Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

SEATTLE -- Amazon.com Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

THOUSAND OAKS, Calif. -- Amgen Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla. -- AutoNation Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Burlington Northern Santa Fe Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

HOUSTON -- ConocoPhillips reports first-quarter financial results.

HOPKINTON, Mass. -- EMC Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

HERSHEY, Pa. -- Hershey Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

FOREST HILLS, N.Y. -- JetBlue Airways Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

BETHESDA, Md. -- Marriott International Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

SACRAMENTO, Calif. -- McClatchy Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

REDMOND, Wash. -- Microsoft Corp. reports third-quarter financial results.

LOS GATOS, Calif. -- Netflix Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- Nucor Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

LOS ANGELES -- Occidental Petroleum Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

PURCHASE, N.Y. -- PepsiCo Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- Philip Morris International Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Raytheon Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

MIAMI -- Royal Caribbean Cruises Ltd. reports first-quarter financial results.

EDEN PRAIRIE, Minn. -- Supervalu Inc. reports fourth-quarter financial results.

OMAHA, Neb. -- Union Pacific Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

ATLANTA -- United Parcel Service Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

TEMPE, Ariz. -- US Airways Group Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

OVERLAND PARK, Kan. -- YRC Worldwide Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

BRUSSELS -- Somalia's prime minister, U.N. Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon and European Union representatives attend anti-piracy donors' conference.

FRIDAY, April 24

WASHINGTON -- Commerce Department releases durable goods for March, 8:30 a.m.; Commerce Department releases new home sales for March, 10 a.m.; Group of Seven finance ministers meet, as do the Group of 20 ministers.

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- 3M Co. reports first-quarter financial results.

ST. LOUIS -- Arch Coal Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

MORRIS TOWNSHIP, N.J. -- Honeywell International Inc. reports first-quarter financial results.

NEW YORK -- Schlumberger Ltd. reports first-quarter financial results.

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Xerox Corp. reports first-quarter financial results.

SATURDAY, April 25

WASHINGTON -- Spring meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank, through April 26.
