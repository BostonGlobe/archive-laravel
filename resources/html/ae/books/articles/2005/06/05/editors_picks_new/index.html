FICTION

The Hot Kid by Elmore Leonard (Morrow)

Our Bodies, Ourselves: A New Edition for a New Era by Boston Women's Health Book Collective (Touchstone)

Salonica, City of Ghosts: Christians, Muslims, and Jews, 1430-1950 by Mark Mazower (Knopf)

Portable Prairie: Confessions of an Unsettled Midwesterner by M.J. Andersen (St. Martin's)
