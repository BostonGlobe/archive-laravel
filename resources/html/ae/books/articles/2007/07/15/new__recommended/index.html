New & Recommended

CHRIS MOONEY (GAIL ALBERT HALABAN)

July 15, 2007

Storm World: Hurricanes, Politics, and the Battle Over Global Warming
By Chris Mooney
Journalist Mooney addresses the debate over a link between hurricanes and global warming with vigilance and verve (Harcourt, $26).

Still Life With Husband
By Lauren Fox
The cute gives way, frequently, to the acute in this intelligent tale of a young woman's infidelity (Knopf, $22.95).

Leviathan: The History of Whaling in America
By Eric Jay Dolin
New England's legends of giant catches and wrecked lives are colorfully recounted in this thorough history (Norton, $27.95).

A Thousand Splendid Suns
By Khaled Hosseini
A heartfelt character study of two women and a lament for the author's native Afghanistan (Riverhead, $25.95).

The Maytrees
By Annie Dillard
Dillard's second novel is an unusually adult kind of love story (HarperCollins, $24.95).
